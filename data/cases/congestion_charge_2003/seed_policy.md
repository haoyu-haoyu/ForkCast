## Policy Description

The central London congestion charging scheme introduced a daily charge for driving or parking a vehicle on public roads within the central London charging zone. The initial scheme described by TfL charged £5 per day during weekday charging hours, excluding weekends and public holidays.

The stated policy aim was to reduce traffic congestion in and around the charging zone, support bus service improvements, improve journey time reliability for car users, and make goods and services distribution more reliable, sustainable and efficient.

## Draft Stakeholder Frame
