# London ULEZ 2023 Expansion Seed Policy

This seed policy describes the 2023 expansion of the London Ultra Low Emission Zone for Phase 1 extraction.
The canonical evidence remains `truth_set.json`; this text is only a compact policy brief for the LLM extractor.

The ULEZ expansion took effect on 2023-08-29 and expanded the zone across all London boroughs and the City of London.
Non-compliant vehicles pay a daily charge, with petrol and diesel compliance standards captured in the truth set.

The case is used to backtest a decision-support simulation against observed political, public reaction, compliance, and air-quality signals.
